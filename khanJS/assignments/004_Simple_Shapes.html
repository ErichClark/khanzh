Challenge: Simple Shapes
挑戰: 簡易形狀

Time to draw your own shapes! Let's go over them here before jumping into more awesome programs.
是時候畫你自己的形狀了!在學會更多超棒的程式之前,讓我們先復習一下。

Step 1: Draw a rectangle, nice and simple!
第一步: 畫一個長方形,輕鬆簡單!

To complete this step, just type in exactly the code you see on the right →.
要完成這一步,把右邊的程式準確地寫下 → 

rect(10, 20, 100, 150);

Step 2: Okay, how about a second rectangle?
第二步: 好,來第二個長方形又怎樣?

Make another rectangle, and fill the blanks with your own numbers! Change the numbers and explore what happens!
再畫一個長方形,在空格裏填上你要的數字!改改數字來看看會發生什麽事情! 

rect(____, ____, ____, ____);

Step 3: Could you draw an ellipse?
第三步: 你能畫一個橢圓形嗎?

Draw your ellipse wherever you'd like! Keep exploring!
把橢圓形畫在任何你喜歡的位置!繼續探索吧! 

ellipse(___, ___, ___, ___);

Step 4: What about a perfect circle?
第四步: 那麼完全圓形呢?

Add another ellipse() that forms a perfect circle. Remember that a perfect circle has its width equal to its height, so be sure to use exactly the same number twice when you say 'ellipse' -- whatever you put in those colored squares, be sure it is exactly the same!
用另一個 ellipse() 來做一個完全圓形。要記得一個完全圓形的高和寬是相等的,所 以當你使用橢圓形指令時要用同樣的數字 -- 不論你在顔色空格裏填了什麽 ,要確定數字都是一樣的! 

ellipse(___, ___,      ,      );

Step 5: Last, a lonely line
第五步: 最後,一條孤獨的線

You got this! Just draw one line anywhere you want!
你行的!在任何地方畫下一條線! 

line(___, ___, ___, ___);
