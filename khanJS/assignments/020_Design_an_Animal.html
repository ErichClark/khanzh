Spin-off of "Project: Design an Animal"
「練習：設計動物」衍生作業

You made lots of animals in the variables coding challenges- a bunny, a mouse, a frog. What other animals can you make with shapes and variables?
在變數編碼挑戰中你畫了許多動物──兔子、老鼠、青蛙。你還可以用圖形和變數畫出什麼動物呢？

Make your animal using the drawing commands, and use variables for the x and y positions, like we do in the starter code below. When you change your x and y variables, you should see your whole animal move.
運用繪圖指令畫出你想畫的動物，並利用變數表示x及y座標，就像我們在下方起始程式碼所做的。當你改變x及y座標的變數時，應該就能看見整隻動物移動了。

You should also use variables for any repetitive information about your animal, like the eye size, so that you can change the size of both of your eyes at once.
動物身上所有的重複資訊都應該用變數處理，像是眼睛的大小，這樣你就可以一次同時改變雙眼的大小。

Starting code:
起始程式碼：

var bodyX = 200;
var bodyY = 220;

fill(244, 255, 163);
ellipse(bodyX, bodyY, 113, 106); // body?
ellipse(bodyX, bodyY-70, 62, 47); // face?
