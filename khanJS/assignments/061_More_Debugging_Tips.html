More Debugging Tips

There are many ways to debug your programs! Here's a list to get you started:
更多的偵錯提示

"Print" debugging:
“列印”偵錯法：

As we just showed, you can insert print()s or println()s into your code, to help you figure out which code is being called and with what values. Those functions both output values in a console that pops up over the canvas. You can also use debug() to send the output to your browser's JavaScript console, if you know how to use that.
如剛剛所示，你可以在程式碼中插入 打印() 或 打印線()，來幫你找出是哪個程式碼被呼叫，及其值為何。以上兩個函數都會輸出對應的數值至畫布上跳出的控制台中。如果你知道如何操作的話，你也可以使用 調試() 把輸出送至瀏覽器的JavaScript控制台中。

"Rubber duck" debugging:
“橡膠小鴨”偵錯法：

Sit a rubber duck next to your computer (or whatever duck-like object you have handy), and explain your program and problem to it, line-by-line. Many programmers find that just the process of putting the problem in words helps their brain realize what's wrong. You can also ask a friend or teacher to "be your rubber duck" - sitting and listening to you explain it. Sometimes they might even think of a solution for you, but regardless, they're doing you a service by just listening to the explanation.
在你的電腦旁放置一個橡膠小鴨（或你手邊有的任何鴨子狀的物體），然後逐行地對它解釋你的程式及問題。許多程式撰寫人員發現，將問題轉化為文字的過程，能幫助大腦發現錯誤的地方。你也可以邀請一位朋友或老師來當你的「橡膠小鴨」－坐下並聽你解釋。有時他們甚至可能提供你解決方案，但無論如何，他們正藉由聆聽你的解釋來幫助你。

Exaggerate your output:
誇大你的輸出：

Since you're making programs in ProcessingJS, you're dealing with lots of fill colors and strokes. When I'm not seeing the visual output I expect, sometimes it helps to use really big or extreme values for the fill and strokes - like strokeWeight(30). Since our environment is real-time and includes the number scrubbers, it's really easy to change up the numbers in your program to see what effect it has on the output. For example, it might help you figure out where a missing shape went to.
因為你於ProcessingJS中編寫程式，你將會碰到各式各樣的顏色填充及筆劃。當我看不到預期的視覺輸出時，有時把填充與筆劃的調到極大值會有幫助－例如 描邊粗細(30)。因為我們的環境是即時且具備數字更動的功能，我們能輕易地更改程式中的數字，來了解它對輸出有何影響。例如，它能夠協助你釐清消失的形狀到哪去了。 

// hard to see, can you see it? 幾乎看不見，你看得到嗎？
// make it obvious with a big stroke 透過大筆劃使其明顯

It's a good idea to get comfortable with all your options for debugging programs, so you can use whichever one works the best in a particular situation.
熟悉你所有的偵錯選項是個不錯的主意，以便你能針對不同的情況選擇最適用的方式。
