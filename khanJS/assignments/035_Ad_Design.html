Spin-off of "Project: Ad Design"
“课题：广告设计”的衍生作品

In the text challenges, you showed that you know how to use text commands. Now, use that knowledge to make an advertisement for a real or made-up product! Suggested steps:
在文本挑战中，你们已了解如何使用文本命令。现在，请使用你们学到的技能来为一款现实或虚构的产品做一个广告。我建议你们参照如下步骤：

  1. Change the big headline to the name of your product.
    将大标题换成你产品的名字。
  2. Add more text advertising the amazing benefits of it.
    添加更多宣传产品功效的文字。
  3. Play around with color, size, and positioning to make it more flashy.
    调整颜色，大小和位置来使它更时尚。
  4. Add a drawing of the product.
    添加一幅产品的手绘。
  5. Bonus: If you want to go really wild, add an animation.
    额外内容：如果你想自由发挥，请制作一部卡通片。
