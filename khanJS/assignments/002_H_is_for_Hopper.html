Challenge: H is for Hopper

Hopper is our beaver that guides you through challenges. Make her happy by drawing an "H"!

Step 1: Start the H!

To draw an H using rectangles, we need two tall ones on the side and a short one connecting them in the middle. Start the first side by typing in exactly the code below. 

rect(80, 70, 60, 240);

Step 2: Now, the right side.

For the right side of the H, we want a rectangle that's exactly the same as the left side, except for the x position. Not sure how to get started? You could just copy the same code that you wrote for step 1, and then try changing the x (the first number) until the second rectangle is on the right hand side. 

rect(80, 70, 60, 240);
rect(     , 70, 60, 240);

Step 3: Connect the sides!

Now add a rect() command after the first two rect()s. The final rect() command will connect the sides with a rectangle in the middle, completing our 'H'. Not sure what numbers to use for this rectangle? Try drawing it on a sheet of paper, or just make up random numbers at first, and change them after until it looks like a middle. 

rect(80, 70, 60, 240);
rect(     , 70, 60, 240);
rect(140, 170,      ,      );
