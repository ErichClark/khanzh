Project: Shooting Star
作業：流星

Now that you've learnt the basics of animation with variables, make a shooting star! We've started off this program with just a star positioned using 2 variables, and it's up to you to decide how to animate it. Here are the steps we suggest:
利用變數現在你學會了動畫製作的基礎，來畫一顆流星吧！程式一開始就給了我們一顆星星，利用兩個變數來定位。你可以隨意創造這個動畫，我們建議以下步驟：

  1. Decide what side of the canvas you want the star to start from, the top, bottom, left or right, and position your star there.
    決定你希望星星從畫布的哪一邊開始，頂端、底部、左邊還是右邊，然後把星星定位。
  2. Decide what direction you want the star to move in. Change the variables inside draw so that the star moves the way you wanted.
    決定你想要星星從哪個方向移進來。改變 draw 裡的變數，讓星星的移動方式如你所希望的那樣。
  3. Add another star or other shape, and animate it going in a different direction.
    加另外一顆星星或其他形狀，使它從不同的方向劃過。
  4. Bonus: Change the star into a different shape, or have it shoot out of a cannon.
    加分任務：將星星改成另一個形狀，或使它從一個大炮發射出來。
  5. Bonus: Add a backdrop, like a starry night or skyscrapers.
    加分任務：加一個背景，例如星空或一些高樓大廈。

Starting code 起始程式碼： 

var xPos = 200;
var yPos = 200;

draw = function() {
    background(29, 40, 115);
    fill(255, 242, 0);
    ellipse(xPos, yPos, 10, 10);
};
